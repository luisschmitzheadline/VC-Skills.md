## Key 2026 Trends to Watch

1. **AI Premium**: AI-native = 2x growth, AI-feature = 1.5x growth
2. **Efficiency Over Growth**: Profitability matters for valuation
3. **Vertical Wins**: Specialized SaaS commanding premium multiples
4. **Usage Pricing**: Shift from seats to consumption
5. **M&A Acceleration**: Lower rates + dry powder = more deals
